About Us

Our Story

WINDOT - a multi-product and multi-dimensional enterprise born with a philosophy to cater the food of the best quality. A tireless effort and hard work of our founder Mr. Navin S. Vaid has brought WINDOT to a new height from scratch. WINDOT has retained services of experts from the field of food processing industries. Mr. Surendrakumar A. Vaid, father of our founder, had passed his whole life in Namkeen business since 1979, who is a hard core first generation entrepreneur as well as a guide to his son. A journey has just begun with this small step with giant ambitions.

The Products

WINDOT has prepared a road map of its journey to reach at the highest level in food sector. As selection and procurement of best quality raw material is the first step of production of the best quality end products, WINDOT has focused all its attention on this vital and basic need of producing products of best quality. The judicial selection of best quality whole and ground cereals and grams is the first priority for WINDOT. Similarly, hygiene of plant, people and premises equally focused followed by timely dispatch of products with personal attention and inspection. This is a philosophy in practice at WINDOT. A small enterprise with big thoughts and big ambitions.
